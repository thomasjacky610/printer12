Common Printer Problems & Quick Fixes

Printer Offline?

  • Restart printer and computer
  • Check Wi-Fi connection
  • Set as default printer
  • Clear print queue

Paper Jam?

Gently remove stuck paper and check rollers. Avoid tearing paper inside.

Not Printing Clearly?

Clean print heads and check ink/toner levels.

How to Choose the Right Printer

  • Home Use: Inkjet printers are affordable and compact
  • Office Use: Laser printers offer faster printing
  • High Volume: Ink tank printers save money long-term
  • Wireless Needs: Choose WiFi-enabled models
